## AI Governance Checklist

Before deploying an AI use case, organizations should ask:

- What business problem does this AI system solve?
- What data does it use?
- Is sensitive or regulated data involved?
- Who reviews the AI output?
- What happens if the AI output is wrong?
- Is the system explainable enough for the use case?
- Are logs and decisions auditable?
- Is there a fallback process?
- Who owns the risk?
- How will performance be monitored?
