# Human-in-the-Loop Governance

Human-in-the-loop governance defines when, where, and how people should review AI output before it is used for decisions, communication, or action.

This is especially important for enterprise AI, legal AI, financial AI, HR AI, compliance workflows, and customer-facing automation.

---

## Why Human Review Matters

AI systems can be fast and useful, but they can also produce incomplete, incorrect, or unsupported outputs.

Human review helps reduce:

- Incorrect decisions
- Legal risk
- Compliance risk
- Reputational damage
- Customer harm
- Over-reliance on AI
- Unclear accountability

---

## Review Levels

### Level 1: User Review

Used for low-risk tasks.

Examples:

- Brainstorming
- Internal notes
- Non-sensitive summaries
- Draft ideas

Control:

- User reads and edits before use

---

### Level 2: Business Review

Used for medium-risk tasks.

Examples:

- Customer email drafts
- Business reports
- Internal policy summaries
- Sales content
- Operational recommendations

Control:

- Business owner reviews before external use

---

### Level 3: Expert Review

Used for high-risk tasks.

Examples:

- Legal document review
- Compliance interpretation
- Financial recommendations
- HR-related decisions
- Contract risk assessment

Control:

- Qualified expert reviews and approves before action

---
